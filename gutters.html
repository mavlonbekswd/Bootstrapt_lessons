Gutter Turlari va O'lchamlari:
  • g-0: Gutterlarni o'chirish
  • g-1 dan g-5 gacha: Har xil o'lchamdagi gutterlar
  • gx-*: Faqat gorizontal gutterlar
  • gy-*: Faqat vertikal gutterlar
  • g-{breakpoint}-*: Responsive gutterlar

O'lchamlar:

  • g-0: 0
  • g-1: 0.25rem (4px)
  • g-2: 0.5rem (8px)
  • g-3: 1rem (16px)
  • g-4: 1.5rem (24px)
  • g-5: 3rem (48px)
